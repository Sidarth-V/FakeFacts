Project Description

We aim to solve the biggest issue that exists in today's world; disinformation and misinformation. With news being easily shareable through social media and the internet, its highly implausible for any source to limit all fake news from entering media and its subsidiaries. It then becomes important for us to stay vigilant and understand what aspects of the news we receive on a daily basis is fake. Our project specifically solves this issue by granting everyone easy access to check their information. We use the Fake news data set from kaggle(link below) and have trained a decision tree classifier model. The project can discern real news articles from the fake ones with an accuracy percentage of 99.5-99.8%.
